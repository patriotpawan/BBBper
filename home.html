Blood-Brain Barrier permeability prediction

Blood-Brain Barrier (BBB) is a selectively permeable physico-chemical barrier that regulates the passage of a multitude of large and small molecules into the microenvironment of the neurons.

Significance of Blood Brain Barrier

  • The main purpose of the blood-brain barrier is to protect the brain from circulating toxins or pathogens, that can cause brain infections, while also allowing essential nutrients to pass through. Although it seems that the BBB is helping our brains by limiting what can go in and out.

  • BBB helps to maintain relatively constant levels of hormones, nutrients and water in the brain and any fluctuations in level of hormones or nutrients could disrupt the finely tuned environment.
